Here is all the buzz about honeybees

The domestic honeybee,is known scientificlly as the European or western honeybee, and is the most common of the nine species of honey producing bees.It is native to Africa,Europe and the Middle East.

Beekeeping or apiculture is the human management of honey bees.These practices date almost to 90,000 years ago.Modern beekeepers typicaly use human made beehives that are boxes with rows of wooden frames that bees fill with wax honeycomb.The hives are designed to help beekeepers contain honey and help maintain the nest.Honeybees produce several product like honey and bees wax,which is why they are so important to humans.
